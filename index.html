Getting There
From Singapore to Kampung Chui Chak
Recommended

Fly Direct to Ipoh (IPH)

The simplest way to get here. Scoot operates daily direct flights from Singapore Changi to Ipoh, with a flight time of around 1 hour 15 minutes.

✈️ Route: SIN → IPH via Scoot
💰 Fare: From ~SGD 300 return (subject to availability)
🕐 Out: ~12:30 PM from SIN (approx. 1h 15m flight)
🔄 Return: ~2:30 PM from IPH (timings vary by date)

From Ipoh to Kampung Chui Chak

It is roughly a 1.5-hour drive from Ipoh Airport to the kampung. You are welcome to rent a car, or join a shared van that we will coordinate with other guests closer to the date.

Alternative

Fly to Kuala Lumpur (KUL)

Fly into Kuala Lumpur, rent a car, and enjoy the drive north. It is about 2 hours from KL and a wonderful option if you would like to explore the city or take in the scenery along the way.

While You’re Here
Explore the Neighbourhood

There is plenty to take in between celebrations. The pace here is gentle, the scenery is lovely, and the local food is absolutely worth trying.

View on Maps ↗

Teluk Intan

Pay a visit to the iconic Leaning Tower and wander the charming heritage streets of this riverside town, just a short drive from the kampung.
View on Maps ↗

Chui Chak Paddy Fields

Take a walk through the paddy fields of 水闸 at golden hour. The views are truly stunning, so do bring your camera.
View on Maps ↗

Ipoh & Cave Temples

Spend some time in Ipoh and explore the limestone cave temples, old-town street art, and some of the best street food in Malaysia.
